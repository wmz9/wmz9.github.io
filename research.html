Research Interests

I am broadly interested in theory, algorithm and application of machine learning. I am also interested in non-convex optimization and image processing. Specifically, my recent research topics are

  • Deep learning theory: optimization, generalization, implicit bias and approximation

  • Foundation models: theory and algorithm.

  • Non-convex optimization: theory and algorithm.
